# EduRecord AI Agents

This document outlines the AI agents involved in the development and maintenance of the EduRecord: Peñaranda Off-Campus Library System.

## Antigravity (Lead Developer Agent)
- **Role**: Lead Software Engineer & Architect.
- **Responsibilities**: 
    - Full-stack development (Java, MySQL).
    - Architectural design and patterns implementation.
    - Code quality and consistency.
    - Documentation maintenance.
    - Design implementation based on the "Academic Excellence & Digital Integration" theme.

1. **Consistency**: Maintain the established `repositories`, `models`, `services`, `ui` structure.
2. **Security**: Ensure secure data handling, especially with MySQL operations.
3. **User Experience**: Prioritize a premium, institutional feel as per the brand identity.
4. **Agility**: Rapid iteration and verification of features.
